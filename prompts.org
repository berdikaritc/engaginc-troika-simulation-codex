*** The game mechanics

P1 starts by taking one card from the draw pile or from the last card discarded
by the previous player. He then discards another (or the same) card, keeping his
hand size at 9 cards.

P2 then does the same, and so on.

Each player is trying to collect 8 cards of the same color or category, for
example 8 red cards or 8 music cards.

As soon as a player has collected 8 cards, he reveals his card and wins. The
game ends.

*** The game rules
At the start of the game, the 81+3 (84) cards are shuffled and then distributed
evenly to all the players into piles, face down.

P1 starts by discarding the top of his pile into the discard pile at the center,
flipping it face up and calling "1 Small".

The next player (in this case, P2) continues with "2 Small".

Then the next player (in this case, back to P1) continues with "3 Small".

After that, "1 Medium", "2 Medium", "3 Medium", then "1 Large", "2 Large", "3
Large", then back to "1 Small" and so on.

When a player has run out of cards, he skips discarding cards.

When the discarded card happens to have the same quantity of object as well as
size of object as the call, or happens to be a wildcard card, players must race
to slap the card as fast as possible. The player who slaps last (whose hand is
at the topmost) must take all the cards in the discard pile, flip to face down,
and add it to the bottom of his pile. Then the turn continues from him,
continuing the call according to the abovementioned pattern.

The first player to discard all the cards in his pile *and* avoids being the
last slapper in the next slap, wins.
