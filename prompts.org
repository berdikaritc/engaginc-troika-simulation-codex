*** The game description
The Troika cards can be used to play many kinds of games.
One of them is Collect Eight.
The object of this game is to be the first two collect 8 cards of the same color or category.
This game utilizes the 81 object cards but none of the wildcards.
Shuffle the cards, deal 9 to each players face down.
Players take turns taking a card from the draw pile or the last card discarded by previous player, then discard 1 card from their hand, to keep their hand at 9 cards.
When a player has collected 8 cards of the same color or category, he reveals his hand and wins.
*** The game mechanics
At the start of the game, each player is dealt 9 cards.

P1 starts by taking one card from the draw pile or from the last card discarded
by the previous player. He then discards another (or the same) card, keeping his
hand size at 9 cards.

P2 then does the same, and so on.

Each player is trying to collect 8 cards of the same color or category, for
example 8 red cards or 8 music cards.

As soon as a player has collected 8 cards, he reveals his card and wins. The
game ends.

Then the game continue from this player.
When a player has emptied his pile, he must still participate in the next slap and avoid being the loser.
The first player that does this, wins.
