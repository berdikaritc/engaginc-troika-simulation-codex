the simulation will be for 2 players (P1 and P2).

** Layout of the screen & appearance
From top to bottom:

- The top logo+information area: the very top will contain title (use logo file
  logo/logo.png), and turn/step information, and nothing else. The logo
  positioning should be absolute so it does not move to the left or right
  whenever the information displayed is changed.
- P1's hand: then P1's hand which will occupy almost 1/3 of vertical area of the
  screen. The cards put in this hand should not overlap or cover the top area or
  the discard pile.
- discard pile: discard pile which will also occupy almost 1/3 of vertical area
  of the screen. The cards put in this pile should not overlap or cover the
  cards in P1's hand or P2's hand.
- P2's hand: P2's hand which will also occupy almost 1/3 of vertical area of the
  screen. The cards put in this pile should not overlap or cover the cards in
  discard pile.

Try to conserve vertical space.

The labels for the areas should not be covered by the cards. Maybe put the
labels far on the right side.

The players' hands will be displayed in accordeon style with fixed spacing between
cards (~1/7 width of the card). The leftmost card will be the bottommost card,
and subsequent cards on the right are cards that are on top of the previous
cards (cards to their left).

The discard pile will be displayed in accordeon style with initial spacing
between cards of ~1/7 width of the card, but as more cards are put into the
discard pile, this spacing will be reduced gradually. When there are 12 to 19
cards on the discard pile, the spacing is reduced to ~1/9 width of the card.
When there are 20 or more cards on the discard pile, the spacing is reduced to
~1/11 width of the card.

** The simulation data
An example simulation data is in simdata/S1/simulation.json file.

The simulation structure specifies list of turns to make. Each turn contains
information of the number of steps to make. And each step contains a summary as
well as list of card moves to make. Each card move is a 3-element array in the
format of [CARDNAME, FROM_PILE, TO_PILE] specifying movement of card from a pile
to another pile.

There are 4 different "piles" in the game:
- P1: P1's hand, which will be shown in an accordeon style to make sure the top
  left part of each card is visible to let viewers see the symbols on the card
  and know what card it is).
- P2: P2's hand.
- DRAW: the draw pile, which is not shown in the display, but when a player is
  drawing from the draw pile, the card will be animated from the left of the
  screen to the P1's hand)
- DISCARD: the discard pile, which is also shown in an accordeon style to let
  viewers identify each card.
